What Is Fssai?

Food safety and standard authority of India is an autonomous body established under Ministry of health, family welfare, Government of India. It was formed under the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006 with sole aim to promote food hygiene and safety in India.

It is responsible for protecting and promoting public health through the regulation and supervision of food safety.

Benefits of FSSAI are

• Consumer Awareness
• Legal Advantage

TYPES OF FOOD LICENSE

FSSAI BASIC LICENSE REGISTRATION

The food business operators like petty food manufacturers and also the small sized manufacturers, storage units, transporters, retails, marketers, distributers are required to obtain Basic FSSAI Registration. It is issued by State Government. It is mostly for units having an annual turnover upto 12 lakh. The maximum tenure is 5 years and minimum is 1 year.

FSSAI STATE LICENSE REGISTRATION

Food business operators like small to medium sized manufacturers and also the small sized manufacturers, storage units, transporters, retails, marketers, distributers are required to obtain FSSAI State Registration license. The state license is issued by state government and

its important that you have operations in only one state like Delhi, in order to obtain State License. It is mostly for units having annual turnover more than 12 lakhs. The maximum tenure of this license is 5 years minimum is 1 year.

FSSAI CENTRAL LICENSE REGISTRATION

Food business operators such as Importers, 100% export oriented units, large manufacturers, operators in central government agencies, air ports, sea ports etc are required to obtain a central Food License. It is issued by central Government. FBOs have to obtain central license for their head office and if they have operations in more than one state. It is mostly for units having an annual turnover of more than 20 crore. The maximum tenure is 5 years and minimum is 1 year.
